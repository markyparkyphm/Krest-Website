Our Story

About Krest Engineers

A certified minority-owned civil engineering firm delivering precision technical services from the heart of Houston, Texas.

Who We Are

Krest Engineers, LLC is a certified civil engineering firm headquartered in Houston, Texas. We provide specialized technical services for public infrastructure, private development, and government agency projects throughout Texas and Louisiana.

As a HUB, DBE, MBE, and SBE certified firm, we are committed to delivering high-quality engineering services with integrity, efficiency, and a deep understanding of local regulatory environments.

Our expertise spans the full project lifecycle — from conceptual planning and environmental permitting through final design and construction support — ensuring clients receive comprehensive, cost-effective solutions.

Our Mission

To provide technically rigorous, client-focused engineering solutions that improve the built environment and support sustainable infrastructure development across Texas and Louisiana.

Our Approach

Every project begins with a thorough understanding of the client's goals, site conditions, and regulatory requirements. We combine local market knowledge with technical expertise to deliver efficient, permittable designs on schedule and within budget.

We maintain direct communication with clients throughout every phase, providing clear documentation, proactive problem-solving, and responsive project management from kickoff to closeout.
