What We Do

Our Engineering Services

Five specialized service areas backed by TxDOT pre-certifications and decades of combined experience in Texas and Louisiana infrastructure.

Civil & Drainage Design

Infrastructure

Our civil and drainage design services cover the full spectrum of stormwater and drainage infrastructure needs. We design systems that efficiently convey runoff while meeting all applicable local, state, and federal requirements — from small commercial sites to large-scale public works projects.

We work closely with municipal clients, TxDOT, and private developers to produce clear, constructible construction documents that streamline the permitting and bidding process.

Stormwater detention basin design
Open channel & culvert design
Storm drain system design
Outfall structure engineering
Erosion & sediment control plans
TxDOT & municipal plan preparation
Drainage area delineation
Construction document preparation

Hydrology & Hydraulics (H&H)

Floodplain & Watershed

Our H&H team provides comprehensive hydrological and hydraulic engineering services, from watershed-scale runoff analysis to detailed floodplain modeling. We apply industry-standard tools including HEC-HMS, HEC-RAS, and SWMM to produce technically defensible studies for TxDOT, municipalities, and private clients.

With TxDOT pre-certifications in hydrologic studies, riverine hydraulics, bridge scour, and FEMA regulations, we are well-positioned to support both design projects and regulatory submittals.

Hydrologic modeling (HEC-HMS)
Hydraulic modeling (HEC-RAS)
Floodplain delineation
FEMA LOMA/LOMR/CLOMR submittals
Bridge hydraulic analysis
Bridge scour evaluations
Storm water pump station design
Watershed & drainage area studies

Site Development & Permitting

Land Development

From raw land to shovel-ready site, Krest Engineers manages the full civil engineering scope of site development projects. We coordinate with surveyors, utilities, environmental consultants, and regulatory agencies to deliver complete, permitted construction packages on time.

Grading & earthwork design
Utility layout & coordination
Paving & pavement design
Subdivision plat preparation
Drainage permit applications
Harris County FCD submittals
City of Houston plan review
USACE Section 404 coordination

Traffic Impact Analysis

Transportation

Our traffic engineers conduct rigorous traffic impact analyses (TIAs) that evaluate the effect of proposed developments on the surrounding road network. We prepare studies that meet the requirements of TxDOT, the City of Houston, Harris County, and other local agencies throughout the region.

Studies include existing and future traffic volume analysis, level-of-service calculations, turn lane warrants, signal warrant analysis, and recommended mitigation measures to support safe, efficient access and operations.

Traffic impact analysis reports
Trip generation & distribution
Level-of-service analysis
Signal warrant studies
Turn lane warrant analysis
Access management planning
TxDOT driveway permit support
Intersection improvement design
