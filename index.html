About Krest Engineers

A Certified Engineering Partner in Houston, Texas

Krest Engineers, LLC is a HUB, DBE, MBE, and SBE certified civil engineering firm based in Houston, TX. We bring technical precision and local expertise to every project we undertake.

Licensed in both Texas and Louisiana, our team holds nine TxDOT pre-certifications covering GIS analysis, hydrologic studies, roadway and bridge hydraulics, storm water design, and FEMA regulations.
